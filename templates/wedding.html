Frequently Asked

What is the dress code?

Indian traditional attire is warmly encouraged. Sarees, lehengas, sherwanis, kurta-pyjamas are all perfect. Day 3 family colour is deep red ‚Äî please avoid wearing bridal red and white.

Are children welcome?

Of course! It wouldn't be an Indian wedding without the little ones. Children are welcome at all events. The hotel has babysitting services available on request for the reception.

Is the food vegetarian?

The wedding ceremony menu is fully vegetarian. The sangeet dinner will have both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. Please note dietary requirements in your RSVP.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We have a professional team capturing everything. During the muhurtam, we kindly request phones away so you can be fully present. Post-ceremony, photograph everything and tag #PriyaAndArjun2025!

Is alcohol served?

The wedding ceremony and lunch are alcohol-free as per tradition. The sangeet and reception will have a full open bar with premium spirits, wine, and mocktails.

What if I can't make all events?

We understand! Indicate which events you'll attend in your RSVP. The wedding ceremony on the 22nd is most important ‚Äî if you can only come to one, let it be that! üôè
